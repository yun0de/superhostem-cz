How to get and connect iCal from Airbnb and Booking

iCal is the fastest way to get your first booking and block dates to Superhost. This tutorial addresses the two most common situations: how to find iCal on Airbnb and how to get it from Booking.com.

Help Center Previous article Next article →
What awaits you next?

First you copy the iCal link from Airbnb or Booking, then it you put in the property settings in Superhost and finally you check that the calendar has been spelled correctly.

What iCal can and can't do

What iCal normally transfers

  • date of arrival and departure
  • busy or blocked appointments
  • basic event identification

What iCal doesn't usually transfer

  • the price of the reservation
  • amount of commission and fees
  • complete guest details

When to use iCal

iCal makes the most sense in the beginning when you want to connect quickly calendar with real estate. If you want to work with more detailed ones reservation data, followed by email forwarding and automation.

What it looks like in Airbnb

How to get iCal from Airbnb

Open Calendar on Airbnb

According to Airbnb's current official help, you start in the section Calendar and select the specific listing you want to connect

Go to Availability

Open the section in the calendar of the given listing Availability. That's where Airbnb is currently hiding connection with other calendars.

Select Connect to another website

Under Connect calendars click on Connect to another website. Airbnb will show you calendar link that you can export.

Copy the entire iCal link

Copy the entire address, not just part of it. Airbnb specifically states that for import on the other hand it should be a URL ending in .ics.

How to get iCal from Booking.com

Open the Booking.com Extranet

Log in to the Booking.com Extranet and open a specific object, that you want to link.

Go to Calendar or Calendar & Pricing

Depending on the current version of the Extranet, run either via Rates & Availability → Calendar, or directly to Calendar & Pricing.

Open Sync calendars

Open in the calendar Sync calendars and select Add calendar connection. This will open Booking wizard for connecting to another calendar.

Complete the calendar export

In the wizard, click on Copy link and copy your Booking.com iCal link. Under Export options it happens it is possible to choose whether you want to export only reserved dates or and closed dates.

Check for correct drive and link status

If you have multiple rooms or units, verify that you are taking iCal right away for the specific property you are connecting. After completion, in An extranet usually displays the connection status, for example Activating or OK.

What can you take away from it practically?

At Airbnb, thanks to the screenshots, we already have the current flow through confirmed Calendar a Availability settings. U Booking.com follow the same logic: open the calendar, find sync calendars and copy the export link for specific unit.

How to insert iCal into Superhost

Open the property detail

In Superhost, you open a specific property that you just you set.

Find the section for calendars or iCal

In the property settings, go to the section where the resource is added calendars.

Paste the iCal URL

Paste the copied address from Airbnb or Booking into the field for iCal and name the source clearly, e.g Airbnb or Booking.

Save the settings and check the synchronization

After saving, verify that the reservation or blocking has been written to property calendar.

What to watch out for

  • always copy the entire iCal link, not just part of it
  • for multiple units, make sure you match the correct calendar to the correct one real estate
  • iCal does not transmit prices or complete financial information
  • if nothing went wrong, check that the link is not invalid or old
  • at Airbnb today, count on the fact that the official route leads through Calendar → Availability → Connect calendars
  • at Booking.com, the names of the sections differ according to the version of the Extranet, but you currently move most often through Calendar, Calendar & Pricing a Sync calendars
If you need not only blocking dates, but also more detailed data about reservations, follows iCal, typically email forwarding and others automation.
